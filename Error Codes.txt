Error Codes
----------------

0 = Sucessful
1 = WDIpad is not opened on the system
2 = WDIpad would not load or could not find the location number box
3 = Could not find the SSH button
4 = Could not find the CC button
5 = Timeout loading CC 
6 = CC did not come up
7 = either issue logging into the cc or getting the page number
8 = could not verify the cc was on the correct page
